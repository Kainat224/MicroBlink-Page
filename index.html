Payment Verification

Seamlessly and securely ensure card-present, high-stakes online transactions

BlinkCard is an AI-powered software solution that reads and digitally captures diverse payment cards, adding a layer of authentication to account funding and online payments, including high-stakes transactions. Mitigate the risk of card-not-present fraud  with AI-powered credit and debit card scanning that is fast, easy, and secure.

Enable Safer Online Transactions with AI

BlinkCard lets users scan any physical payment card in real time, on-device, to add it to an account or use it for a one-time purchase. BlinkCard is powered by machine learning models trained for unique tasks, like detecting and capturing a card issuer or other relevant data fields on payment cards.

Mitigate card-not-present payment fraud

Card-not-present fraud is responsible for over 75% of credit card fraud globally, and has only increased in the last few years in our growing digital economy. For businesses, the losses associated with credit card fraud are more than just financial, damaging brand reputation and fracturing trust.  Reduce card-not-present fraud with BlinkCard. Our AI-powered payment card scanning software integrates into existing mobile and web applications to automatically detect and capture data from credit and debit cards issued by Visa, Mastercard, and ten other global networks. BlinkCard snaps clear, high-resolution images of both sides of a payment card, which can be later used to verify that the card is genuine and belongs to the user.

Preserve consumer experience across high-stakes, time-sensitive transactions

Credit card fraud is bad for businesses and customers alike, with card-not-present fraud costing US consumers nearly $10 billion annually alone. 
Boost security without adding friction to your user experience by integrating BlinkCard’s AI computer vision software into your mobile or web applications. Whether it’s funding a crypto account, transferring money between institutions, or purchasing tickets to a once-in-a-lifetime event, users simply snap a pic of both sides of their payment card and our technology does the rest. 
Automatically detect and populate data from more than 1,700 credit and debit cards, strengthening your payment verification workflows while eliminating manual data entry. With Microblink, frictionless user experience and best-in-class security are no longer mutually exclusive.

Secure, on-device processing

BlinkCard runs directly on your user’s mobile device or your own servers, without the need for an internet connection. As an out-of-scope system, Microblink has no access to the cardholder data environment (CDE). Reduce your PCI compliance scope and feel confident that user PII is not being shared by third parties — without sacrificing card-not-present fraud mitigation. 
With custom field extraction, BlinkCard can be tailored to populate info from specific fields only, like card number, expiration date, and CVV — the required data for the majority of online purchases. In addition, BlinkCard can be configured to mask certain parts of the payment card (in results and on cropped images) to comply with relevant data privacy laws.
